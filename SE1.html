SE1 Electrolyte Analyzer | POC Portable ISE Analyzer

The Seamaty SE1 handheld electrolyte analyzer is specifically designed to enhance patient care across various healthcare settings. Its compact and easy-to-use design makes it perfectly suited for use at the patient's bedside in hospitals, within private clinics, urgent care centres, and even in rural health posts or community pharmacies. Its portability empowers healthcare workers to perform on-the-spot electrolyte tests without relying on distant laboratories. This reduces the need for sample transport, speeds up diagnosis, and ensures patients receive timely care, especially in areas with limited resources.

Key Features & Benefits

- Multi-Parameter Testing: Measures Na+, K+, Cl-, Ca2+
- Low Sample Volume: Requires only 80-120 µL per test.
- Versatile Sample Types: Accepts whole blood, serum, plasma, or aqueous solution.
- Automatic System Functions: Auto calibration, cleaning, and standby mode.
- Convenient Design: Touchscreen interface and built-in thermal printer.
