SMT-120 Clinical Biochemistry Analyzer

A compact, intelligent, and fully automated clinical chemistry system crafted for precision diagnostics right at the point of care. Designed to empower small and medium-sized laboratories, the SMT-120 delivers high-accuracy test results in just 12 minutes, minimizing delays and maximizing workflow efficiency. With a sleek, space-saving design and ultra-simple operation, it’s the perfect solution for healthcare professionals who need speed without compromising on quality. Each test run utilizes our proprietary single-use reagent disc—a sealed innovation that contains up to 18 essential biochemistry assays, eliminating cross-contamination and reducing waste.

Key Features & Benefits

- Fast Turnaround: Delivers accurate results within 12 minutes.
- Zero Maintenance: No pump, no valve, no fluid tubing-calibrate-free.
- Minimal Sample Volume: Requires only 100µL of whole blood, serum, or plasma.
- Compact Footprint: Lightweight, and space-saving for maximum flexibility.
- User Friendly Touchscreen: 6.5-inch color display with intuitive software.
- Multiple Test Panels: Supports up to 16 biochemical reagent discs covering liver,kidney lipid,glucose, and electrolyte panels.

Technical Specifications

Sample Types: Lithium Heparin whole blood, serum, plasma
Sample Volume: 100µL
Test Time: Approx. 12 minutes
Resolution: 0.001 Abs
Absorbance: 0-3.0 Abs
Testing Principle: Absorption Spectroscopy, Turbidimetry
Testing Method: End Point, Kinetic, Two Point
Light Source: 12V/20W Halogen lamp with shelf live over 2500 hours
Test Panels: Up to 16 different reagent panels(pre-installed rotors)
Display: 7-inch color touchscreen
Printer: Built-in thermal printer
Storage: 500,000 test results
Interfaces: USB
Weight: 4.2kg
Power Supply: AC 100-240V, 50/60Hz
