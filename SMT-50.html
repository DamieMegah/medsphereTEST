SMT-50 | 5-Part Auto Hematology Analyzer

The SMT-50 Seamaty 5-part auto hematology analyzer is a compact, all-in-one solution that combines simplicity and reliability through advanced laser scattering technology. Its space-saving design includes a built-in reagent cabinet, while the intelligent anti-blockage feature ensures a seamless user experience. With precise results driven by a superior algorithm, high-quality hardware, and optimized liquid circuit design, it delivers high performance at an ultra-low operating cost using only three reagents. Supporting both 3-part and 5-part analysis, it processes up to 60 samples per hour with minimal reagent and sample consumption.

Key Features & Benefits

- 5 Part WBC Differentiation: Measures lymphocytes, monocytes, and neutrophils, eosinophils, and basophils.
- High Throughput: Up to 60 test per hour.
- Compact Footprint: Ideal for small labs.
- User-Friendly Interface: 10.4-inch colored LCD touchscreen
- Low Sample Volume: 20µL (whole blood), 40µL (pre-diluted).
- Built-in Thermal Printer: Quick report output.
